A Casetta

A CHARMING APARTMENT ON THE GROUND FLOOR.

Able to accommodate up to 4 people, the apartment has two beautiful bedrooms with garden views, each with its double bed, a bathroom with shower, a large living room with fireplace, as well as a spacious kitchen.
It also has a large private terrace with direct access to the garden and the swimming pool area. The outdoor facilities at your disposal are, a garden table, deckchairs as well as a barbecue. All you have to do is enjoy the peace, nature and surrounding activities.

Practical information: Bed and bath sheets are provided on site and car spaces are available on the property.

The little extra to disconnect: The house has no internet connection, no excuse to continue working remotely, here we are on vacation! If however an internet connection is essential to you, you have an info point located in the center of the village where you can connect for free.
